Open Console

Right-click first. Then press "Inspect" to open console. You will see the word "Console" right next to "Elements". You will click on that to access the console.



You can add things in console through writing a script. You need to put "console.log, some varibles (var ___ (your choice of naming it), and finally displaying the output like this: "console.log("variable's name"). Lastly, you must have a semicolon after everything in the script.
